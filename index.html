:: PURPOSE (User: Learner) ::

AI is a tool that requires understanding to be used properly. This site provides methods to make AI more accessible and safe, empowering users to get the most out of simple pattern predictors.

NORTH STAR: AI dependence erodes depth of understanding. Use it to amplify inquiry, not bypass it.

1.0 SYSTEM ETHICS

Privacy

Be mindful of sharing personal data, copyrighted material, or sensitive information (medical, financial, government records, etc.).

Copyright

AI trains on scraped data. Don't feed it protected content.

Labor/Env

High energy cost. Exploitive filtering labor. Prompt judiciously.

Bias

Outputs reinforce training biases. Always PROMPT-check.

Card 01 - PREPARE

3.0 ADVANCED PROTOCOLS

Protocol 1: Chain of Thought (CoT)

Boosts accuracy by forcing logic. Add this line:

"Think step-by-step before answering."

Protocol 2: Adversarial Playoff

Use for structured debate:

Persona 1 (Optimist): Argue for the proposal.
Persona 2 (Skeptic): Argue against it.
Persona 3 (Judge): Debate, then summarize valid points.

:: AI USE CATEGORIES (NON-NEGOTIABLE) ::

  • [CAT 1] No AI allowed.
  • [CAT 2] Allowed—cite per policy, appendix with record.
  • [CAT 3] Required—acknowledge critically.

PROMPT HEURISTIC PROTOCOL

Mandatory verification layer for all AI outputs.

Ref Criterion Domain Check
P Presentation Polished prose? Ignore aesthetics if content is weak.
R Relevance Matches your question/scope? (e.g., specific context)
O Objectivity Balanced views? Diverse voices (Global South)? Your biases?
M Method Transparent process? Did CoT show steps? Sample diverse?
P Provenance Traceable to primary sources? Avoid fabricated citations.
T Timeliness Current debates? (e.g., 2025 AI ethics in healthcare?)

REFLECTION: The Final Gate

Skip reflection, and it's just procrastination. Ask yourself:

  1. Why this tool?
  2. Losing key learning?
  3. Risks (bias, time)?
  4. New skills gained?
