AI_GUIDE_v1.1

A quick workflow for using AI without outsourcing your brain.

:: PURPOSE (User: Learner) ::

AI generates salience, not truth. It is a "confident autocomplete" that provides what sounds right, not necessarily what is true. We offer the methods to navigate this gap, helping you use AI as a tool for deeper understanding rather than a shortcut to an unexamined life.

NORTH STAR: AI dependence erodes depth of understanding. Use it to amplify inquiry, not bypass it.

1.0 SYSTEM ETHICS

Privacy

Rule: Never share personal data or sensitive information.

Example: Don't input medical records, financial details, or government documents.

Copyright

Rule: Don't input protected content you don't own.

Example: Avoid pasting full articles, books, or proprietary code.

Labor & Environment

Rule: Use prompts efficiently to reduce resource use.

Example: Combine related questions into one well-structured prompt.

Bias

Rule: Assume outputs reflect training biases and verify.

Example: If a claim feels one-sided, check other perspectives.

2.0 THE AI CYCLE LOOP

Card 01 - PREPARE
[Clarity]

Goal: Decision, learning, or production.

Self-check: Am I replacing learning I should do myself?

Card 02 - PROMPT
[Direct]

Formula: Persona + Context + Constraints.

Example: "Act as a writing coach. Help me outline an essay about AI ethics for a college audience. Keep it under 500 words."

Card 03 - VERIFY
[Check]

Test claims, don't trust tone.

Cross-check key facts with primary sources.

Card 04 - REFLECT
[Reflection]

Risks: Bias, hallucination, over-reliance.

Skill practiced: Critical evaluation, research.

3.0 ADVANCED PROTOCOLS

Protocol 1: Visible Checkpoints

Boosts accuracy by making reasoning explicit. Request:

- List your assumptions.
- Show any quick calculations.
- Identify 3 failure modes.
- State confidence level and what evidence would change your answer.

Protocol 2: Adversarial Playoff

Use for structured debate:

Persona 1 (Optimist): Argue for the proposal.
Persona 2 (Skeptic): Argue against it.
Persona 3 (Judge): Debate, then summarize valid points.

Required output: Decision, strongest pro, strongest con, and what evidence would settle it.

PROMPT HEURISTIC PROTOCOL

Mandatory verification layer for all AI outputs.

PROMST = Presentation, Relevance, Objectivity, Method, Sources, Timeliness.
Ref Criterion Questions to Ask
P Presentation Is the content strong, or just polished prose?
R Relevance Does this match my actual question and scope?
O Objectivity Are multiple perspectives represented, including marginalized voices?
M Method Is the reasoning process transparent and checkable?
S Sources Can I verify claims with primary sources, or are citations fabricated?
T Timeliness Is this information current for my context?

REFLECTION: The Final Gate

Mandatory question: What decision or skill am I working on?

Optional checks:

  1. What do I lose by outsourcing this?
  2. What would falsify the AI's claim?
  3. What's the smallest next action without AI?
